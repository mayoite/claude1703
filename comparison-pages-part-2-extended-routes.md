This section should be treated as highly valuable even though it is narrow.

## Part 2 conclusion

The extended route family reveals a repeating pattern:

- many routes are directionally right
- too many are operationally under-finished
- several utility, proof, and support pages weaken trust more than they strengthen it

The strongest findings in this part are:

- remove or fix `/tracking`
- turn downloads into real document delivery
- clarify proof-route roles
- deepen category/PDP decision support
- either build or honestly collapse placeholder solution pages
- treat social, support, and legal surfaces as credibility surfaces rather than low-priority extras
